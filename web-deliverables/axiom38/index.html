What We Build

Every major institution — the VA, school systems, higher education — operates through rules, terminology, and procedural frameworks that individuals struggle to navigate alone. Axiom 38 builds software that bridges the gap between personal experience and institutional logic, giving people the knowledge they need to advocate for themselves.

Our Products

Three distinct platforms serving veterans, parents, and students — each tackling a different institutional language barrier.

🎖️

Decode38

Free web app that helps veterans and transitioning service members understand VA disability claims — translating their lived experience into the regulatory language the VA uses to evaluate claims.

  • VA disability rating estimator (38 CFR Part 4)
  • Plain-language to VA terminology decoder
  • C&P exam preparation guidance
  • AI-powered personal statement builder
  • 26 medical condition modules
  • 100% free — no paywall, no account required

Tech Stack

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript · Claude Sonnet API · Zero dependencies · Client-side only

Visit Decode38

PlanVocate

Web platform that empowers parents to advocate effectively for their children in IEP and 504 meetings — bridging the gap between institutional education language and what parents actually need to know.

  • IEP & 504 eligibility screener
  • Personalized meeting preparation guide
  • Know-your-rights resource library
  • Communication templates & letter builders
  • Built by educators, for parents

Tech Stack

Node.js/Express · Netlify Serverless Functions · HTML/CSS/JS · Claude API

Principia

iOS app that teaches mathematics the way it actually works — unified, visual, and grounded in the why behind every formula. From 6th-grade fractions through university calculus, every concept connected across disciplines.

  • 8 tiers: 6th grade through Calculus II
  • Cross-discipline concept connections
  • Live visualizations for every concept

Our Thinking

Our work is grounded in a simple observation: institutional complexity is a tax on the people least equipped to pay it.

Institutional Language

How systems like the VA and school districts develop specialized terminology and procedural logic that excludes people unfamiliar with the framework — and how software can bridge that gap.

Systems Translation

Designing software layers that convert complex institutional rule structures into human understanding — without losing the critical nuance that determines real outcomes.

Complexity as Barrier

Why procedural complexity often produces inequitable outcomes, and how better information design — software that translates, not just explains — can reduce it.
