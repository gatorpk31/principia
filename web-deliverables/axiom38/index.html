Why This Matters

The VA, school systems, and universities operate on rules and language most people were never taught. That gap costs veterans their benefits, parents their voice, and students their confidence. Axiom 38 closes that gap with software — not consultants, not jargon, not gatekeepers.

Why We Built This

Axiom 38 was founded by a disabled veteran. He learned that the systems meant to serve people — the VA, schools, universities — only work for those who already know the rules. We build software to change that.

For Veterans

Every veteran deserves to understand their rating, file their claim, and get their benefits without hiring someone to translate the VA's language.

For Parents

Parents know their child best. They deserve to walk into IEP meetings as equals, knowing exactly what to ask for and why.

For Students

Math isn't isolated formulas. It's a connected way of thinking. Students deserve to understand the why behind every concept.
