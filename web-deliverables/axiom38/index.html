What We Build

The VA, school systems, and higher education all operate on rules and terminology most people were never taught. That gap costs veterans their benefits, parents their voice, and students their confidence. Axiom 38 closes that gap with software — not consultants, not jargon, not gatekeepers.

Our Products

Three products. Three audiences. One mission: give people the knowledge to advocate for themselves.

🎖️

Decode38

Free web app that helps veterans and transitioning service members understand VA disability claims — translating their lived experience into the regulatory language the VA uses to evaluate claims.

  • Estimate your VA disability rating before you file
  • Translate your symptoms into VA claim language
  • Prepare for your C&P exam with confidence
  • Build a personal statement with AI assistance
  • Covers 26 of the most common service-connected conditions
  • 100% free — no account, no paywall, no catch
Visit Decode38 →

PlanVocate

Web platform that empowers parents to advocate effectively for their children in IEP and 504 meetings — bridging the gap between institutional education language and what parents actually need to know.

  • Find out if your child qualifies for an IEP or 504 plan
  • Walk into any meeting knowing what to ask for
  • Understand your rights under IDEA and Section 504
  • Generate letters and communication templates
  • Built by educators who know how these meetings really work
Visit PlanVocate →

Principia

iOS app that teaches mathematics the way it actually works — unified, visual, and grounded in the why behind every formula. From 6th-grade fractions through university calculus, every concept connected across disciplines.

  • Covers 6th grade through university calculus in one app
  • Every concept taught visually — not just memorized
  • Shows how ideas connect across algebra, geometry, and calculus
  • Guided walkthroughs at your own pace
  • Free to start — no credit card required

Why we build this

Axiom 38 was founded by a disabled veteran who learned firsthand that the systems meant to serve people — the VA, school districts, universities — only work well for those who already know how to navigate them. We build software to change that.

For Veterans

The VA claims process shouldn't require a VSO, an attorney, or years of trial and error. Decode38 gives every veteran the knowledge to file with confidence.

For Parents

Schools speak a language most parents were never taught. PlanVocate gives parents the words, rights, and preparation to show up as equals at the table.

For Students

Mathematics isn't a collection of formulas to memorize — it's a connected way of thinking. Principia teaches the why, so the how actually sticks.
